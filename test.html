Welcome

Bay Area catering services

Servers Standing By Catering offers top of the line corporate catering services from San Francisco to the Silicon Valley. Based in San Mateo, California, we have proudly been a San Francisco catering company and Silicon Valley catering company for over 15 years. Our specializations include corporate events such as daily business luncheons, multiple day retreats, and conventions.

 

Here at Servers Standing By Catering, we take pride in serving only the finest, lovingly prepared foods to our Bay Area clients. Our seasoned, passionate chefs take full advantage of California's fresh-grown produce, meats, and seafood, serving only the absolute best quality food to our highly-valued customers. We believe that our San Francisco catering customers deserve natural foods that are served with the utmost care, which is why we exercise green business practices daily.

 

We provide excellent service to our Bay Area clients, striving to deliver the perfect dining experience. We achieve this by extending just as much courtesy and care to our employees as we do to our clients. We simulate a family-environment here at Servers Standing By, extending our family to yours and tending to all your San Francisco and Silicon Valley catering needs.
